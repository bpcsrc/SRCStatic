Partners

The Socially Responsible Computing project is a collaborative effort between 6 college campuses: Cal State Fullerton, Cal State LA, Cal Poly Pomona, University of California San Francisco, Cal Poly San Luis Obispo, and Cal State University Dominguez Hills. Computer Science professors from each campus work together to create the best curriculum material for advancing social reform through computer science.
