Faculty Learning Community

BPC SRC personnel hold biweekly Faculty Learning Community (FLC) workshops dedicated to empowering educators with the tools and insights needed to integrate socially responsible computing into their classrooms and foster the next generation of computer scientists. It is not only important that educators equip their students with technical skills, but that students also gain a sense of social responsibility.

Our workships provide a platform for teachers to explore innovative approaches, share best practices, and engage in meaningful discussions about incorporating ethical considerations into their computing curricula. From addressing issues of digital equity to promoting inclusivity in technology, our sessions aim to inspire educators to create learning environments that reflect the values of responsible and ethical computing.
